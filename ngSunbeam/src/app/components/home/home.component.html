What is Sunbeam?

Sunbeam connects families, caretakers, and senior citizens. Whether in permanent care, temporary care, or simply staying with other family members, Sunbeam provides a centralized space for all involved parties to coordinate care and appointments.

Communication

Communicate with other family members or caretakers. Leave notes for the entire group to inform them of an event or to simply say hello to your loved one.

Protected Data

Medical records, medications, and medicine logs are confidential and unable to be viewed by site moderators. Your data stays with you.

Planning

Set appointments for the client that the entire group can track. Set medication or event reminders that your group can track.
